About




Hi and welcome to my page! My name is Ellen Pieterson and I am a Software Developer. At school the subjects I enjoyed the most were those which had a problem solving element, such as Mathematics and Physics. This passion led me to studying a degree in Engineering Business Management at the University of Warwick. It was here that I had my first taste of using code to tackle a problem and I've been hooked since!

My 12-week _Nology journey to becoming a Software Developer has been nothing short of amazing; in such a short period of time I've have become a more confident developer and professional. This website details some of my experiences, please explore the page and feel free to let me know what you think using the contact information at the bottom of the page!
